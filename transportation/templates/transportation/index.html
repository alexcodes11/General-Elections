Do Your Local Politicians Believe in Public Transportation?

I built an algorithm that displays whether or not they mention any form of public transportation on their website!! Of course this project is a work in progresss. I only have a couple of states at the moment. The reason being is because I still want to debug this application and learn how to use Selenium Python! Once I am done with that I will start adding all 50 states through my algorithm! Click on the Button's below to get started...

Lastly, I wanted to explain what it means to believe in public transportation. Well, when I think of Public Transportation we think of some few key words. For me it's a variety of travel options like buses, subways, and light-rail, trains, biking infrastructure, and pedestrian friendly infrastructure. My algorithm looks for these specific words through Python Regex and BeautifulSoup. My algorithm needs some more work on it, but it is also worth to mention that a lot of politicians do not have public transportation as a priority.
