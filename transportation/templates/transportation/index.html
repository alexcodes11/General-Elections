Lastly, I wanted to explain what it means to believe in public transportation. Well, when I think of Public Transportation we think of some few key words. For me it's a variety of travel options like buses, subways, and light-rail, trains, biking infrastructure, and pedestrian friendly infrastructure. My algorithm looks for these specific words through Python Regex and BeautifulSoup.
